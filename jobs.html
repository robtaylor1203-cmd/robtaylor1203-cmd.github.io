Head of Tea Buying

The London Tea Company · London, England

Job Description

The London Tea Company is seeking an experienced and passionate Head of Tea Buying to lead our global sourcing and quality control team. This is a senior leadership role responsible for maintaining the exceptional quality of our existing tea portfolio while identifying new and exciting teas and botanicals from around the world.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and execute the global sourcing strategy for all teas and herbal infusions.
  • Manage relationships with tea gardens, estates, and suppliers worldwide.
  • Lead the quality control and cupping team to ensure all products meet our high standards.
  • Travel internationally to origin countries to meet with producers and inspect crops.

Qualifications:

  • 10+ years of experience in the specialty tea industry.
  • Expert-level palate and tea tasting skills.
  • Proven track record of international sourcing and supplier management.
